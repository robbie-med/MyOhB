Call Your Provider If…
During Pregnancy
  • Vaginal bleeding
  • Fluid gushing or trickling
  • Baby not moving normally
  • Severe headache
  • Vision changes
  • Sudden swelling of face/hands
  • Fever >100.4°F
  • Contractions before 37 weeks
After Delivery
  • Soaking >1 pad/hr × 2 hrs
  • Fever >100.4°F
  • Wound opens or oozes
  • Severe headache
  • Leg redness/swelling
  • Chest pain or trouble breathing
  • Thoughts of self-harm
